I am a Staff Battery Scientist at Blue Current, working on developing next generation solid state batteries, with a focus on silicon anodes. My PhD is from the University of California, Berkeley where I was a member of the Balsara Lab, a member of the Joint Center for Energy Storage Research (JCESR) Solid Solvation Thrust, and affiliated with Lawrence Berkeley National Lab. My graduate research focused on the ion transport and thermodynamic properties of polymer blend electrolytes for application in lithium batteries and beyond. More broadly, my interests encompass energy storage, alternative energy solutions, and optimization of processes. Please contact me via email if you would like to collaborate, brainstorm ideas, or learn more about my work.

In my free time, I volunteer as an assistant coach and chief financial officer for Born to Run Track Club, a non-profit youth track club based in Mountain View, CA.
